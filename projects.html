My Projects

These are a few of my works throughout my journey in growing as an artist and in college, each artwork is special and created with all the effort I can give. Some of my projects are personal projects that I wanted to explore my skills and see what I can achieved by making own designs and asking for critiques every now and then.

Echoes of Yin and Yang: The Rise of the Phoenix and Dragon

A concept artbook that I created as a Final project from one of my Projects in FEU. It is about love triumphing over greed, and balance of the world is restored, heralding a new era of peace and prosperity for the kingdom.

Ruddy Rune Project

Hazbin Hotel Pins
