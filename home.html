Ruddy Rune

Ruddy Rune is the brand name that I created during my 1st year in FEU. The name RuddyRune comes from what I thought about connecting it to fantasy and creativity as I love creating Artworks as if it is Magic.

Illustations is one of my strongest forte, creating art pieces whether an original character I'll draw it whether may it be simple or complex.

TRADITIONAL PAINTING
